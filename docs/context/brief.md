# Syner Cortex - Context Brief

## 1. Project Background
Syner Cortex is an AI-powered Enterprise Consulting Operating System designed for startups, SMEs, corporate executives, and Syner consulting agents. 

The platform turns unstructured internal documentation, key business processes, and operational KPIs into structured business diagnostics, cited semantic answers, and execution roadmaps.

## 2. Antecedent: ARIANA (2019)
Syner Cortex represents the modern cloud-native evolution of **ARIANA (Artificial Intelligent Analyst Assistant)**, originally conceived in 2019 by Humberto Arturo Villanueva Alvear. 
While Ariana was an early audio assistant skill concept, Syner Cortex upgrades this strategic vision into a full multi-tenant SaaS workspace, analyzing operations across four key corporate dimensions:
- **Commercial & Sales (Ventas)**
- **Operations & Delivery (Operaciones)**
- **Finance & Administration (Administración/Finanzas)**
- **Human Resources & Talents (Recursos Humanos)**
- **Technology & digital maturity** (new dimension added for modernization)

## 3. Commercial Positioning
Syner Cortex is marketed as a comprehensive consulting SaaS layer:
> **"Tu consultor estratégico con IA, entrenado con el contexto real de tu empresa."**
> **"De documentos dispersos a decisiones ejecutivas."**
